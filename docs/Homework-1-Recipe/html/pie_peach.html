Carrera's Blackberry Peach Pie

One 9-inch pie
15 Min - Prep time
50 Min - Cook time
65 Min - Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups fresh blackberries
  • 3 fresh peaches - peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 double crust ready-to-use pie crust
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Mixing: Mix the blackberries, peaches, cornstarch, and sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Make Pie: In a 9-inch pie pan ...
    • Press one of the pie crusts into the bottom of a 9-inch pie pan.
    • Pour the blackberry mixture into the pie crust.
    • Cover with the remaining pie crust.
    • Crimp the edges of the two crusts together to seal.
    • Cut slits in the top of the pie to vent.
    • Brush the top with the melted butter.
    • Sprinkle the cinnamon and nutmeg over the top.
  4. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes. Reduce the oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and continue to cook until top crust is golden brown, 35 to 40 minutes.
